20 Ideas for Perfect Holiday Gifts: Get Ready on Time!

November usually brings colder weather, less sunlight, and the beginning of a period when we retreat into our warm homes. The holiday atmosphere is sneaking around the corner, a time spent with family and friends, sharing love, joy, and happiness. We slowly start to think about gifting our loved ones, but preparing for the holidays can be challenging, especially when facing an increasing number of obligations.

The Benefiti platform allows you to easily find the perfect gifts for your loved ones, and that too without stress. During November, you can choose gifts on the platform that will be available starting December 1st. Here are 20 ideas that will surely delight your loved ones.

Note: Some services are available only in certain cities.

Spend quality time together

The best gift for our loved ones is the time we spend together. We can fill that time with interesting activities, and here are some offers you can find on our platform:

  1. Tickets for events: Gift an experience of special moments with vouchers for Tickets.rs, where you can reserve tickets for concerts, museums, or theaters.
  2. Bowling and fun: Spend quality time with family and friends at the bowling alley Žabac, where you can enjoy bowling, darts, billiards, table tennis, and video games. Available in Novi Sad and Belgrade.
  3. Escape room adventure: Experience the excitement of interactive Escape Room games at Get Out or PIN Escape. Available in Novi Sad and Belgrade.
  4. Paintball: Gather your team and test your skills at the paintball club Warzone in Niš.
  5. Shooting: Improve your shooting skills at Streljačka družina Pančevo 1813 (Pančevo) or Tiron shooting range (Belgrade).
  6. Cinema: If you are in Novi Sad, don’t miss your favorite movies, with popcorn, at Cineplexx Promenada cinema.

Creative gatherings with loved ones

You can surprise your loved ones with a joint trip to a creative workshop. Have you always wanted to try pottery or painting and share these moments with someone?

  1. Creative workshops: Try your hand at pottery at a ceramic mug or bowl-making workshop at Studio keramike Bardak (Novi Sad and Belgrade).
  2. Painting with wine: Express your creativity at painting workshops with a glass of wine at Paint & Wine workshops in Belgrade.

Gifts for the youngest

Children are the most excited about the holidays and gifts, so make sure to provide them with unforgettable moments.

  1. Dexyco vouchers: Kids can get whatever they want at Dexyco stores across Serbia or order online to have it delivered to your address.
  2. Courses for young explorers: For older kids, consider interactive courses at Kliker center, focusing on the basics of computer programming, robotics, and digital creativity in major cities in Serbia.
  3. Children's swings: For the youngest and cutest, you can order baby swings from Ljuljaška.

Innovative gifts

If you want to be different and original, you can give tech lovers one of the following interesting suggestions.

  1. Video box: A digital album for your best memories, with the ability to add up to 2000 digital photos.
  2. Urbigo: An automated garden that grows everything from spices to mini vegetables and forest strawberries for you.
  3. Digital card Tapni: A perfect gift for friends who follow the latest trends.

Gifts for development and entertainment

Books can be the perfect gift for those who enjoy learning and personal development or spend their free time reading.

  1. You can find the best books on success and development at the publishing house Finesa. A limitless selection of all genres is offered by Delfi bookstores, which are located in many cities across Serbia. If you are in Novi Sad, visit Kontrolnikaraktera.
  2. Gift them a subscription to an audio or video library that suits their interests.
