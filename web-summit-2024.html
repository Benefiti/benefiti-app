Benefiti is going to Web Summit 2024 in Lisbon! 🎉

With great excitement, we want to share incredible news – Benefiti has been selected as one of 24 startups from Serbia that will participate in the prestigious Web Summit 2024 in Lisbon! 🇵🇹 This is a huge honor for us and a confirmation that our effort, dedication, and vision are recognized on a global level 🌍.

What is Web Summit and why is it important? 🤔

For those who are not familiar, Web Summit is one of the largest technology events in the world 🌐. It gathers thousands of innovative startups, investors, tech companies, and professionals from various industries. Attending this event gives us a chance to showcase ourselves to a global audience, connect with potential partners and investors, and learn about the latest trends in the tech world 🚀.

Our role in the Serbian delegation? 🇷🇸

Thanks to the support of the Serbian Chamber of Commerce and the Center for Digital Transformation, we will be part of the Serbian delegation that will have its own booth at the Web Summit. Our presence will not go unnoticed, as all Serbian startups will be gathered on the "Serbian Island," which will provide additional visibility and facilitate networking with global players 🌎.

Why is this important for Benefiti? 💡

This is an incredible opportunity for us to present Benefiti to the world 🌍, connect with international clients and investors, and enhance our offering. Web Summit is where key contacts are formed, and we believe this event will be a turning point for our further growth and development 📈.

Social events and networking opportunities 🤝

In addition to the fair, we will be part of exclusive social events 🥂, including a traditional Serbian dinner 🍽️ and a reception at the Serbian embassy in Lisbon. This will allow us not only to build business contacts but also to showcase our culture in a more relaxed atmosphere 🎉.

What do we expect from Web Summit? 🌟

We expect to return enriched with new knowledge 📚, experiences, and contacts 📇. Our mission remains the same – to create innovative solutions that will improve the well-being of employees in companies, and Web Summit is the right opportunity to expand our idea to a global level 🌐.

Thank you all for your support so far – let’s move forward together, step by step, toward achieving our goals 🎯!
