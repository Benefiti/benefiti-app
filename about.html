How Benefiti Were Created

History

Benefiti.app is a project that emerged from the years of experience of its partners in the fields of HR, IT, and business.


The synergy of the best aspects from each of these industries drives the desire for companies to have quality, motivated, and engaged employees. It involves leveraging new technologies as tools to simplify and automate processes, along with a clear business rationale for achieving better results with smarter resource utilization.

The Benefiti.app platform was initiated in response to the specific challenges that employers face concerning employee benefits programs and the issues of attracting, retaining, motivating, and engaging employees.

This platform offers a unique "one-stop-shop" approach to creating personalized, flexible, and measurable benefits programs, all within a budget that is easy for employers to manage.
