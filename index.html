How does the platform work?

A simple and logical way for the platform to function, ensuring your comfort while working.

01. Divide employees into "classes"

Divide employees into "classes" based on the budget you will provide for their benefits (e.g., juniors, seniors, management, upper management…).

02. Allocate budgets for each "class"

Determine the gross budget for each defined class that will not be exceeded.

03. Approve which benefits are available.

Make the final selection of benefits available for each class. Currently, there are over 50 different benefits on offer.

04. Enjoy the analytics!

That's it! The platform will allow each employee to choose benefits on a monthly basis and connect them directly with vendors, while you can focus on analyzing the large amount of data that the platform will generate.
